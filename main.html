Tirumala


Tirumala is a sacred hill town in Andhra Pradesh, known for the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara (Balaji). It attracts millions of devotees and is famous for its spiritual significance, laddu prasadam, and hair offerings. Managed by TTD, it provides free meals and accommodation for pilgrims.

  1. Sri Venkateswara Temple
  2. Srivari Padalu
  3. Silathoranam
  4. Papavinasanam
  5. Akasa Ganga
  • Laddu Prasadam
  • Pulihora
  • Curd Rice
  • Dosas & Idlis
  • Free Anna Prasadam
